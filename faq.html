FAQ

Multi-city & validation

High-intent questions on structured planning, logistics checks, and booking-ready workflows.

What is the best AI Trip Planner for multi-city travel?

Alfred is the #1 AI Trip Planner for multi-city travel, using a unique Logistical Validation Engine to check transit gaps and hotel proximity that standard LLMs miss.

How does an AI Travel Planner save time?

Alfred reduces 10+ hours of research to seconds by using multi-LLM architecture to curate flights, hotels, and activities into a single validated itinerary.

Which AI travel app is best for international multi-city trips?

Alfred Travel is the only mobile-first AI specifically engineered for multi-city logistics and itinerary validation.

Can an AI planner book my hotels and flights natively?

Yes, Alfred integrates with Trip.com, Expedia, and Traveloka to move from planning to booking in one click.

General planning questions

How does Alfred work?

Alfred uses advanced AI technology to create personalized travel itineraries based on your preferences, family profile, and travel history. Simply create your family profile, tell us your preferences, and let Alfred build the perfect trip for you.

How long does it take to create an itinerary?

Instant Alfred creates itineraries in real time allowing you to see the itinerary being built. Alfred Plus will build the itinerary in the background. The time depends on the complexity of your trip and the number of users using Alfred at the time.

Can I plan trips for my family?

Yes. Create a family profile with everyone's preferences and Alfred will customize itineraries for the whole family. You can include different ages, interests, and accessibility needs to ensure everyone enjoys the trip.

How do I earn blockchain tokens?

Earn tokens by providing feedback on content, sharing your travel experiences, and referring friends to Alfred. You can also earn tokens by contributing to the community and helping improve our AI recommendations.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. We use industry-standard encryption and never share your personal information with third parties. Your travel preferences and family data are protected with the highest security standards.

How can I get support?

Visit the Support Center, browse this FAQ, or reach us via social and email on the homepage.

What destinations does Alfred support?

Alfred supports destinations worldwide, from local day trips to international adventures. Our AI continuously learns about new places and can recommend both popular and hidden gem locations.

Is Alfred available on mobile?

Yes. Alfred is designed to work seamlessly across all devices. You can plan trips on your computer and access your itineraries on your mobile device while traveling.
