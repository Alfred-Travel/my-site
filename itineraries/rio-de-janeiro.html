3-day preview

A condensed look at how Alfred structures a Rio de Janeiro trip—arrival gateway, neighborhoods, and daily pacing.

Day 1 — Arrival & Orientation
• Arrive at GIG; cab or bus to Copacabana (validated 45–60 min)
• Check-in; lunch at Confeitaria Colombo or beach kiosk
• Afternoon: Copacabana Beach; Ipanema; Christ the Redeemer (pre-book)
• Evening: dinner at Aprazível or Zaza; Lapa samba

Day 2 — Core Experiences
• Morning: Sugarloaf Mountain (cable car; Pão de Açúcar)
• Lunch: Bar do Arnaudo or Galeto Sat's
• Afternoon: Santa Teresa; Escadaria Selarón; Lapa Arches
• Evening: dinner at Olympe or CT Boucherie; Leblon

Day 3 — Deeper Exploration
• Morning: Tijuca Forest; Parque Lage; Jardim Botânico
• Lunch: Feira de São Cristóvão or Bar Urca
• Afternoon: Maracanã (stadium tour) or Niterói
• Evening: farewell dinner in Ipanema; pack for departure
