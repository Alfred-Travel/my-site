3-day preview

A condensed look at how Alfred structures a Istanbul trip—arrival gateway, neighborhoods, and daily pacing.

Day 1 — Arrival & Orientation
• Arrive at IST or SAW; Havalimanı metro or cab to Sultanahmet (validated 45–90 min)
• Check-in; lunch at a kebab house or balık ekmek
• Afternoon: Hagia Sophia, Blue Mosque, Hippodrome
• Evening: dinner in Karaköy or Kadıköy; Bosphorus stroll

Day 2 — Core Experiences
• Morning: Topkapi Palace (opening 9am; 2–3 hrs)
• Lunch: Sultanahmet Köftecisi or Çiya Sofrası
• Afternoon: Grand Bazaar; Spice Bazaar (Egyptian Bazaar)
• Evening: dinner in Beyoğlu; Taksim Square, İstiklal Caddesi

Day 3 — Deeper Exploration
• Morning: Bosphorus cruise or Princes' Islands
• Lunch: fish restaurant in Bebek or Ortaköy
• Afternoon: Basilica Cistern; Chora Church or Balat
• Evening: farewell dinner in Nişantaşı; pack for departure
