3-day preview

A condensed look at how Alfred structures a Vancouver trip—arrival gateway, neighborhoods, and daily pacing.

Day 1 — Arrival & Orientation
• Arrive at YVR; Canada Line to Downtown (validated 25 min)
• Check-in; lunch at Japadog or Granville Island
• Afternoon: Stanley Park; seawall; Gastown
• Evening: dinner at Miku or Hawksworth; Granville Street

Day 2 — Core Experiences
• Morning: Capilano Suspension Bridge or Grouse Mountain
• Lunch: Lonsdale Quay or Grouse
• Afternoon: Lynn Canyon; Queen Elizabeth Park; VanDusen
• Evening: dinner in Yaletown; False Creek

Day 3 — Deeper Exploration
• Morning: Whistler day trip or Sea to Sky
• Lunch: Whistler Village or Squamish
• Afternoon: return; Kitsilano Beach; Museum of Anthropology
• Evening: farewell dinner in Coal Harbour; pack for departure
