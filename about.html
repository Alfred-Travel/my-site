Our mission

Why Alfred exists

Travel planning moved from agents who chose everything for you, to hours lost in tabs and spreadsheets. Alfred is built for what comes next: structured itineraries you can validate, edit, and book—not one-off chat answers that never become a real trip.

  • Validate — multi-LLM checks on flights, hotels, and day-by-day pacing
  • Plan — one editable itinerary from idea to execution
  • Book — partner paths when you are ready to commit

Give travellers their time back—days of research compressed into minutes of structured planning.
